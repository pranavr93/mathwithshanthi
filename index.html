Sample 1 Mark Questions

1) How many digits after the decimal point will the decimal expansion of 26 / 6250 terminate?

Answer: 5

2) If the product of the zeroes of the polynomial ax2 - 12x - 6 is -3, what is the value of ‘a’?

Answer: a = 2

3) Find the value of ‘k’ for which the system of equations has infinitely many solutions. 2x - 3y = 7 and (k + 2)x - (2k + 1)y = 3(2k - 1)

Answer: k = 4

4) Find the value of ‘p’ for which the quadratic equation 2px2 - 8x + p = 0 has equal roots.

Answer: p = ±2√2

5) What is the common difference of an A.P. in which a21 - a7 = 84?

Answer: d = 6

6) The perimeter of two similar triangles ABC and LMN are 60cm and 48cm respectively, if LM = 8cm, what is the length of AB?

Answer: AB = 10cm

Sample 1 Mark Questions

7) The midpoint of the line segment joining the points (a, 2) and (3, 6) is (2, b). Find ‘a’ and ‘b’.

Answer: a = 1; b = 4

8) Find A if √3sec2A = 2 where 0 < 2A < 90o

Answer: A = 15o

9) If a tower 30m high, casts a shadow 10√3m long on the ground, then what is the sun's altitude?

Answer: 60o

10) From a point of Q, the length of the tangent to the circle is 24cm and the distance of Q from the center is 25cm. What is the radius of the circle?

Answer: r = 7cm

11) The radii of two cylinders are in the ratio 2 : 3 and their heights are in the ratio 5 : 3. Find the ratio of their volumes.

Answer: 20 : 27

12) The mean and median of the same data are 24 and 26 respectively, what is the value of the mode?

Answer: 30
